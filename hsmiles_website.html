About Us

As the CEO of H. Smiles in Miles Safaris, I lead a passionate team committed to delivering unforgettable travel experiences. We specialize in car rentals and custom safari tours, ensuring every client enjoys the beauty of Africa safely and comfortably. Our values of reliability, safety, and personalized service guide every journey.

Our Services

  • Car Rentals: Choose from 4x4s, vans, and saloon cars — with or without a driver.
  • Chauffeur Services: Let our experienced guides take the wheel while you enjoy the view.
  • Safari Packages: Custom tours to top destinations in Kenya and beyond.
  • Corporate Rentals: Flexible long-term solutions for businesses and professionals.

How We Work

Clients make prior bookings through our online system or by contacting us directly. Each vehicle is thoroughly inspected and serviced before departure. We prioritize safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction on every trip.
